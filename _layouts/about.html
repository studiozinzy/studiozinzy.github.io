I’m a learner and sometimes I make things about what I learn. Since I was a child, the Internet has been a medium I use to do so.

I'm a Santomean-Dutch queer person who grew up in the Dutch South. These days, home is Amsterdam. There, in its finest neighborhood, de Indische Buurt, I share a shoebox with Anja, and soon also puppy Lemonade.

In my spare time I like to make music, swim, write, cook, read short stories and comics, enjoy the city, and explore cinema. I like to teach myself new things, and currently I’m diving head-first into minimalist compositions for the piano.

For 16 years I’ve had the pleasure of making a wild variety of things for the Internet.

Drawing from training in research, linguistics, literature, and — yes — theology, I current work as a Design Manager at edtech scale-up Leeruniek.

I love dreaming big and working small to improve habits, teams, products, and services. Being a catalyst for better collaboration and design thinking is possibly my favorite thing in the world.
